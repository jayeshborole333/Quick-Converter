Centimeter

Definition: A centimeter (cm) equals one-hundredth of a meter.

History/origin: Comes from the metric system. “Centi” indicates a division by 100.

Current use: Commonly used in everyday measurements such as the length of a notebook, a pencil, or a handspan.

Kilometer

Definition: A kilometer (km) equals 1000 meters.

History/origin: Introduced with the metric system in France. “Kilo” represents one thousand.

Current use: Widely used around the world to measure distances between geographical locations (e.g., cities, roads).
