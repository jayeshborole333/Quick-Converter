Centimeter

Definition: A centimeter (cm) is equal to 1/100th of a meter.

History/origin: Originates from the metric system. The prefix “centi” signifies a factor of one hundred.

Current use: Used in daily life and scientific contexts for small-to-medium length measurements.

Meter

Definition: The meter (m) is the SI base unit of length, defined by the distance light travels in a vacuum in 1/299,792,458 seconds.

History/origin: First defined in the 1790s; refined based on physical standards and later on the speed of light.

Current use: Universally used in most countries for distance and height measurements.

How to Convert Centimeter to Meter

1 m = 100 cm

Example: convert 250 cm to m:

250 cm = 250 × 0.01 = 2.5 m
